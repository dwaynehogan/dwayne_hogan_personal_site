Monthly SEO Ops & Reporting

Overview

A systematic approach to SEO that treats it as an operational discipline—not a one-time project. This framework turns scattered optimization efforts into a repeatable monthly playbook backed by executive-level dashboards.

The Problem

Most SEO efforts fail because they lack structure:

  • Reactive firefighting — Chasing rankings drops without a systematic response
  • No visibility — Leadership can't see what's working or what needs attention
  • Inconsistent execution — SEO happens when someone has time, not on a cadence
  • Wasted effort — Optimizing the wrong pages while high-impact opportunities sit idle

The Solution: The Monthly SEO Playbook

A systematic framework that runs monthly "plays" based on performance data. Instead of random optimization, you execute specific tactics triggered by specific conditions.

The Six Plays

Play 1: Rankings Recovery

Trigger: A tracked keyword has dropped in rankings.
Objective: Recover lost rankings through systematic optimization—internal linking, meta tag refresh, content updates, FAQ sections.

Play 2: Content Rewrite for Non-Ranking Pages

Trigger: A page is not ranking in the top 30 for its target keyword.
Objective: Complete strategic rewrite to establish ranking potential.

Play 3: Striking Distance Optimization

Trigger: Keyword ranking in positions 4-10.
Objective: Push into top 3 for significantly higher CTR. Small improvements here yield massive traffic gains.

Play 4: Page 2 to Page 1 Promotion

Trigger: Keyword ranking in positions 11-20.
Objective: Break onto page 1 (page 2 gets less than 1% of clicks). Strategic internal linking and content depth expansion.

Play 5: Top Page Amplification

Trigger: Page identified as top performer (top 20% driving 80% of results).
Objective: Maximize value by using top pages to lift other pages through strategic internal linking.

Play 6: Re-Index Deindexed Pages

Trigger: Page not indexed in Google.
Objective: Get the page indexed through Search Console submission, redirects, or full rewrites.

The Command View Dashboard

A monthly executive dashboard that provides complete visibility into SEO performance:

Rankings Overview

  • Keywords in Top 3
  • Keywords in Top 10
  • Keywords on Page 2 (opportunity)
  • Keywords not ranking (risk)

Traffic Metrics

  • Organic sessions (total and by objective)
  • Organic conversions (demo requests, signups)
  • Top landing pages

Content Performance

  • New pages published
  • Pages optimized
  • Plays executed and results

Monthly Actions

  • Rankings declines to address (Play 1)
  • Striking distance opportunities (Play 3)
  • Page 2 promotion candidates (Play 4)
  • Top pages to amplify (Play 5)

Why This Works

  1. Data-driven decisions — Every action is triggered by performance data, not guesswork
  2. Systematic execution — Monthly cadence ensures consistent progress
  3. Executive visibility — Leadership can see exactly what's working and what's not
  4. Focus on leverage — Effort goes where it has the highest impact
  5. Repeatable process — New team members can execute the playbook without tribal knowledge

Results

This operational approach transforms SEO from a mysterious art into a predictable engine:

  • Faster recovery from rankings drops
  • Higher conversion rates as pages move from position 5 to position 1
  • Better resource allocation by focusing on high-leverage opportunities
  • Increased confidence from leadership seeing clear metrics and progress

What's Next

Continuing to refine the playbook:

  • AI-assisted play execution for faster turnaround
  • Predictive analytics to identify opportunities before they become problems
  • Automated alerts for play triggers
  • Enhanced dashboard visualizations for pattern recognition
