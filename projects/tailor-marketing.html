Tailor Marketing Transformation

Overview

Building a comprehensive Notion-based marketing operations system to streamline content creation, project management, and cross-functional collaboration at Tailor.

The Challenge

Marketing teams often struggle with:

  • Scattered information across multiple tools and platforms
  • Lack of visibility into project status and priorities
  • Inefficient workflows that slow down content creation
  • Poor collaboration between marketing, product, and design teams

The Solution

I designed and implemented a unified Notion workspace organized around three strategic pillars:

1. Content/SEO

Drive organic traffic through strategic content creation and SEO optimization.

Key Components:

  • Content Calendar with full pipeline visibility
  • SEO keyword tracking and performance metrics
  • Content brief templates and AI-assisted workflows
  • Integration with freelance writers and editors

2. Conversion Optimization

Optimize website experience to convert visitors into leads and customers.

Key Components:

  • A/B testing tracking and results
  • Lead magnet development and performance
  • Website UX improvement backlog
  • Conversion funnel analysis

3. Social Media

Build brand awareness and engagement through strategic social presence.

Key Components:

  • Social content calendar
  • Platform-specific strategy docs
  • Performance tracking and insights
  • Thought leadership initiatives

Key Features

Unified Project Log

A central tracking system that provides visibility across all marketing initiatives, with:

  • Status tracking (Needs Scope → In Progress → Done)
  • Owner assignment and accountability
  • Area tagging for cross-functional visibility
  • Integration with granular task management

Content Pipeline Management

A sophisticated content workflow that handles:

  • AI-assisted brief creation
  • Freelancer collaboration
  • Multi-stage review processes
  • Publication and indexing tracking

Integrated Task System

Granular task management that:

  • Links directly to parent projects
  • Supports hierarchical organization
  • Enables clear owner assignment
  • Tracks dependencies and blockers

AI Prompt Library

Reusable AI prompts for:

  • Project planning and scoping
  • Content brief creation
  • Decision framework application
  • Workflow optimization

Results

The system has transformed how we work:

  • Improved visibility - Everyone knows project status at a glance
  • Faster content creation - Streamlined workflows reduce bottlenecks
  • Better collaboration - Clear handoffs between team members
  • Scalable processes - Systems that grow with the team

Tech Stack

  • Notion - Primary workspace and database system
  • Notion API - Custom integrations and automations
  • AI Tools - Content assistance and workflow optimization

Lessons Learned

Building an effective marketing operations system requires:

  1. Start with strategy - Organize around business goals, not tools
  2. Design for users - Systems should enable, not constrain, creative work
  3. Iterate constantly - Workflows evolve; systems must adapt
  4. Balance structure and flexibility - Enough process to scale, enough freedom to innovate

What's Next

Continuing to refine and expand:

  • Enhanced automation for routine tasks
  • Deeper analytics integration
  • Cross-platform workflow improvements
  • AI agent assistance for content planning
